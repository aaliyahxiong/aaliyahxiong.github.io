Shopping

There are so so so many places to shop in Korea. Malls are where I would recommend for higher and luxury brands, but there are different areas known for shopping.

Seongsu

This area is popular for holding various pop-up stores for a multitude of brands. This is very popular for their shoe street as well, but this area is most known for its shopping and cafes.

Hongdae

This area is very well known and fun to shop at as there are stores everywhere but I find it to be a little overwhelming. It does have everything though, so I would reccomend this place because of its variety of brands and shopping.

Apgujeong Rodeo Drie

This area get its nickname as Korea's "Beverly Hills" due to its luxury shopping in this area. Many celebrities are known to visit this area and cafes are very well known here as well. There are so many! So if you are unable to find seating at one there are a multitude nearby.

Yeouido

There are lots of malls in this area! The Hyundai mall in this location is very large and houses a variety of brands where they also have a floor dedicated to pop ups.
