Top 3 Restaurant Recommendations

I wanted to have some variety so these are some of my top restaurants!

Pyeongidambaek Bone Noodle Soup Seongsu Branch
Located in Seongsu, this is a restaurant that specialized in their knife cut noodles dish Kalguksu. This restaurant in particular is known for their Bone Broth.
Link to the location
Yeonggwang Napa Wraps with Pork
This is a traditional-style restaurant where you sit on the floor and typically eat with others. The popular dish here is Bossam, boiled pork sliced to be paired with side dishes and lettuce.
Link to the location
Musoe Kimchi Grilled Pork Belly Myeongdong Branch
While there are plenty of KBBQ restaurants in particular, I like this place because their kimchi was distinguishingly better than any other place I had gone to before. The customer service has also always been good. The location is also very popular for tourists to visit.
